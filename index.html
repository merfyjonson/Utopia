Placing Members First

Mountain America Credit Union opened its doors in the 1930s with a simple goal—to help our fellow members financially. Today, that spirit of cooperation still drives us. As a not-for-profit financial institution, our focus is to guide members toward financial success. We do this through a combination of affordable products, friendly service, community involvement and financial education. At Mountain America, you have access to a full suite of financial services, including savings accounts, auto loans, checking accounts, credit cards, SBA loans, business checking, retirement planning and more. Plus, your accounts are always close at hand with our mobile app, online banking and more than 100 branches, spanning Utah, Idaho, Montana, Nevada, Arizona and New Mexico.

As your financial partner, we’re dedicated to preparing you for the road ahead. Let’s make the journey together.
